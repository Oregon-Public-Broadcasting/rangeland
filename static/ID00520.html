The Indian Meadows allotment spans 5,891 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bob And Carol Bruce Grazing Association Llc based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 174 cattle between June and October
  • Mackenzie Family Trust C/O Bob Mackenzie based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 71 cattle between June and October

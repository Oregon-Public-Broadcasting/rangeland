OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Ffr Flynn allotment spans 2,780 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jjf Ranch Limited Partnership based in Plush, OR grazes up to 7 cattle between March and February
  • Jack And Breda Flynn based in Plush, OR grazes up to 6 cattle between May and June
  • Njn Flynn Investments Llc based in Lakeview, OR grazes up to 2 cattle between March and February

The Henderson Ridge Comm allotment spans 1,154 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • David S. Mcdonald based in Grand Junction, CO grazes up to 40 cattle between June and October,
  • Brackett, Todd B. (Parsons Lease) based in De Beque, CO grazes up to 10 cattle between June and October

The Big Creek allotment spans 3,752 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Big Creek Hay & Grange #5 based in Ketchum, ID grazes up to 5 horses between May and October, 50 cattle between July and September, 85 cattle between May and June and 85 cattle between September and October
  • Raymond Whittier based in May, ID grazes up to 5 horses between May and October, 50 cattle between July and September, 85 cattle between September and October and 85 cattle between May and June

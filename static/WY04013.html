The Hickey Mountain allotment spans 6,566 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Dr Land, Llc based in Mountain View, WY grazes up to 36 cattle between May and October
  • Hickey, Jack based in Lonetree, WY grazes up to 77 cattle between May and September
  • Hickey Trust, Jack And Marian based in Lonetree, WY grazes up to 55 cattle between May and October

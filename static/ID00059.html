The Minnie allotment spans 4,707 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Herbert Jones based in Midvale, ID grazes up to 54 cattle between May and October
  • Wayne Cada based in Caldwell, ID grazes up to
    • 71 cattle between May and October
  • Soulen Livestock Co based in Weiser, ID grazes up to 1 sheep between May and June
  • Motichka Farms Inc based in Midvale, ID grazes up to 18 cattle between May and October

The Ridgedale allotment spans 5,510 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bingham Brothers Sheep Company based in Honeyville, UT grazes up to 2,645 sheep between May and June
  • Ward, Kenneth W based in Malad, ID grazes up to 45 cattle between May and September, 528 sheep in October , 110 cattle between May and June, 17 cattle between May and August and 852 sheep between May and June
  • Ward, Ryan based in Malad, ID grazes up to 198 sheep in October , 55 cattle between May and June, 462 sheep in October , 105 cattle between May and August and 330 sheep between May and June

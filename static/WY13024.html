The Big Sandy allotment spans 59,140 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Gz Livestock Company based in Eden, WY grazes up to 13 cattle between May and September,
  • G&E Livestock Company based in Farson, WY grazes up to 2,550 sheep in December , 2,420 sheep in May ,
  • Midland Dunton Sheep Company based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 4,270 sheep in November ,
  • Pretty Water, Llc based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 1,200 sheep in May , 1,200 sheep in November ,
  • Griffin, Christopher M. And Regina M. based in Farson, WY grazes up to 244 cattle between May and October
  • Taliaferro Living Trust, Thomas S. Iv And Linda based in Green River, WY grazes up to 244 cattle between May and October

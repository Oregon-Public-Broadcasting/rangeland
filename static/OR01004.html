OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Durkee allotment spans 9,154 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Shook & Van Arsdale based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 34 cattle between April and October
  • M.R. King Ranches Inc. based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 63 cattle between April and October
  • Bunch Lease based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 36 cattle between April and October
  • Rutherford Lease based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 32 cattle between April and October

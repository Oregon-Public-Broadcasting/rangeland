The Curly Hollow allotment spans 22,241 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Canyonland Grazing Corporation based in Flagstaff, AZ grazes up to 23 cattle between November and May
  • Larson, Blaine Larson Cheryl Ann based in Hurricane, UT grazes up to 49 cattle between November and May
  • Lorraine J. Wittwer based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to 50 cattle between November and May
  • Kyle D Frei based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to 50 cattle between November and May and 57 cattle between November and May

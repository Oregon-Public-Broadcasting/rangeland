The Toquerville allotment spans 5,742 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Delynn C. & Charlene H. Barton based in Hurricane, UT grazes up to 10 cattle between January and February and 10 cattle between March and May
  • Calvin J. & Adeline J. Rollins based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 4 cattle between January and February and 4 cattle between March and May
  • Van H. Kleinman based in Toquerville, UT grazes up to 16 cattle between January and February and 16 cattle between March and May

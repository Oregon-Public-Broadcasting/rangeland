The Lower Basin allotment spans 2,894 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Emil L. And Aubree B. Herbst based in Tendoy, ID grazes up to 157 cattle between May and June and 116 cattle between June and September
  • Swanson, Clarence (Estate) based in Tendoy, ID grazes up to 32 cattle between June and September and 153 cattle between May and June

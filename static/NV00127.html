NEVADA

The Buffalo Hills allotment spans 440,982 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Chuck, Andrea, & Grover Jackson based in Gerlach, NV grazes up to 613 cattle between April and October
  • Jola Ann Mott based in Gerlach, NV grazes up to 21 cattle between April and October

The Shoestring allotment spans 2,281 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Diamond T Ranch Llc based in Brigham City, UT grazes up to 8 cattle between June and October
  • Rogers, David based in Rupert, ID grazes up to 1,450 sheep in May
  • Circle E Ranch Llc based in Mccammon, ID grazes up to 8 cattle between June and October
  • Sheldon Hatley based in Arimo, ID grazes up to 17 cattle between June and October

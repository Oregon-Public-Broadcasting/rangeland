OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Big Butte allotment spans 21,802 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • James And Renee Nevin based in Eagle Point, OR grazes up to 178 cattle between April and June
  • Ron Anderson based in Eagle Point, OR grazes up to 74 cattle between June and October and 179 cattle between April and June
  • Merton Bradshaw Co based in Eagle Point, OR grazes up to 235 cattle between April and May

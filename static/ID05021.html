The Middle Hill allotment spans 2,647 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Hutchison, Jeff & Jodene based in Malta, ID grazes up to 45 cattle between May and June and 20 cattle between June and August
  • Douglas H. Ward based in Almo, ID grazes up to 100 cattle between May and June and 100 cattle between October and November
  • Brent Jones based in Almo, ID grazes up to 72 cattle between May and June
  • Alvin & Mary Carpenter based in Elba, ID grazes up to 19 cattle between May and June and 6 cattle in July
  • Lee Bradshaw based in Caldwell, ID grazes up to 14 cattle between July and October and 19 cattle between May and October

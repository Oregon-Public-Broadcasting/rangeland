OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Iron Mountain allotment spans 4,809 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Winkle Lease/Charles Kester based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 11 cattle between June and November
  • Flack Lease/32 Ranch based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 81 cattle between April and December
  • Wright Lease/Charles Kester based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 8 cattle between September and December
  • Schuck Lease based in Durkee, OR grazes up to 50 cattle between October and December and 50 cattle between April and June

NEVADA

The Ruby Valley allotment spans 20,081 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Barrick Cortez, Inc. based in Elko, NV grazes up to
    • 10 cattle between March and April and 10 cattle between November and February
  • Raymond & Sandy Rosenlund based in Ruby Valley, NV grazes up to 82 cattle in March and 82 cattle between November and February

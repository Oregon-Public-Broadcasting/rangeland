OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Virginia Valley allotment spans 16,242 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • 3 J Land Llc based in Burns, OR grazes up to 216 cattle between April and August
  • Mccoy Creek Ranch based in Diamond, OR grazes up to 100 cattle between April and August
  • Broken Circle Co based in Hines, OR grazes up to 157 cattle between April and August

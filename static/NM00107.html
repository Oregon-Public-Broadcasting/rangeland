NEW MEXICO

The Summers Community allotment spans 9,387 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Zumwalt Revocable Living Trust based in Quemado, NM grazes up to 6 horses between March and February and 153 cattle between March and February
  • Thomas Summers based in Silver City, NM grazes up to 3 horses between March and February and 34 cattle between March and February
  • Badger Creek Corporation based in Springerville, AZ grazes up to 57 cattle between March and February

OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Keeney Creek allotment spans 59,439 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Donald & Susan Coleman based in Harper, OR grazes up to 238 cattle between April and October
    • 160 cattle between April and October
  • Brett & Kerry Marchek based in Harper, OR grazes up to 15 cattle between November and February and 2 cattle between April and October
  • Marchek And Son Inc based in Harper, OR grazes up to 612 cattle between April and October

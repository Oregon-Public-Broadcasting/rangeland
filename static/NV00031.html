NEVADA

The Buttermilk allotment spans 23,512 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Kenneth Buckingham based in Paradise Valley, NV grazes up to 942 cattle between April and May
  • Thomas Grazing Llc based in Paradise Valley, NV grazes up to 391 cattle between April and May

The Onaqui Mountain East allotment spans 24,210 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Rockin R Ranch A Llc based in Rush Valley, UT grazes up to 185 cattle between May and September
  • Johnson Land And Livestock L.L.C. based in Rush Valley, UT grazes up to 11 cattle between May and September

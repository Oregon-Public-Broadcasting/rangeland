The Elephant Cove allotment spans 7,604 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Cox, Jeffery F. (Foote Lease) based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 17 cattle between July and November
  • Tyler And Vanessa Baird based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 70 cattle between July and November

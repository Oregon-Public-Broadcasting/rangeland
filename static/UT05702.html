The Spring Canyon allotment spans 4,270 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mathew & Rayni Harper based in Levan, UT grazes up to 12 cattle between May and October
  • Lynn Jones based in Spanish Fork, UT grazes up to 32 cattle between June and August
  • Paul Mcpherson Investments Llc based in Nephi, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between June and August
  • Tom Goble based in Nephi, UT grazes up to 1,582 sheep in May
  • Randy Revoir based in Nephi, UT grazes up to 60 cattle between June and December

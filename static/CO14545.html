COLORADO

The Cross Creek allotment spans 6,637 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Sutherland Ranch based in Saguache, CO grazes up to 245 cattle between June and July and 245 cattle between September and October
  • Ward Ranches, Inc. based in Saguache, CO grazes up to 73 cattle in October

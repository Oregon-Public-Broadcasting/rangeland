ARIZONA

The Beaver Dam Slope allotment spans 30,623 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Frei, J Claud & Sons based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to 67 cattle between October and February and 67 cattle in March
  • Shelby Frei based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between October and February and 30 cattle in March
  • Dennis Frei based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to 97 cattle between October and February and 97 cattle in March

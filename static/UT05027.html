UTAH

The Ibapah allotment spans 46,632 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Parker Family Ranch Llc based in Ibapah, UT grazes up to
    • 155 cattle between April and July
  • Bateman Ranch L.L.C. based in Ibabpah, UT grazes up to 135 cattle between October and February
  • Knight Land & Livestock (Coalt Lse) based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 377 cattle between November and February
  • Ld Ranching based in Ibapah, UT grazes up to 42 cattle between May and July

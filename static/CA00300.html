CALIFORNIA

The North Ash Valley allotment spans 17,465 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Deforest Family Trust based in Adin, CA grazes up to 361 cattle between May and September
  • Betty Ann Carroll based in Adin, CA grazes up to 178 cattle between May and September
  • Deforest Ranch based in Adin, CA grazes up to 154 cattle between May and September

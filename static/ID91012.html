The South Milner allotment spans 9,224 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mark & Mashelle Barlow based in Jerome, ID grazes up to 38 cattle between April and October
  • Mussmann Berwyn based in Eden, ID grazes up to 86 cattle between April and October
  • James R Grant based in Hazelton, ID grazes up to 2 horses between May and October, 47 cattle between April and October

The Doty Mountain allotment spans 56,238 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Southern Cross Ranches based in Windsor, CO grazes up to
    • 500 cattle between April and December
  • Ray & Kathleen Weber based in Baggs, WY grazes up to 10 horses between March and February, 140 cattle between April and December

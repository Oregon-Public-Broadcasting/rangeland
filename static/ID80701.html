The Crater Butte allotment spans 6,688 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Charles Ritter based in Shoshone, ID grazes up to 17 cattle between April and August
  • James B. Ritter based in Richfield, ID grazes up to 99 cattle between April and August
  • Oneida Farms, Inc. based in Jerome, ID grazes up to 206 cattle between April and August
  • Marjorie Simpson based in Dietrich, ID grazes up to 38 cattle between April and August

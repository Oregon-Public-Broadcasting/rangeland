The Happy Creek allotment spans 95,126 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. The one permit issued for this allotment was not renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Happy Creek, Inc. based in Littleton, CO grazes up to 500 cattle between April and August and 272 cattle between October and February

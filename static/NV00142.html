NEVADA

The South Buffalo allotment spans 229,587 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Goemmer L & L Buffalo Ranch Llc based in Battle Mountain, NV grazes up to 743 cattle between March and February
  • Vesco Ranch based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 15 cattle between April and November

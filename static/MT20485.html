MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Axolotl Lakes allotment spans 2,427 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Martin, Larry & Troy based in Twin Bridges, MT grazes up to 154 cattle between July and October and 250 cattle between June and November
  • T L Irrigation Company based in Hastings, NE grazes up to 135 cattle between June and September
  • Elser & Sons Ranch based in Sheridan, MT grazes up to 200 cattle between July and October

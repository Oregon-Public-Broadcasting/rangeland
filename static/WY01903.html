The Silver Creek Common allotment spans 32,941 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Poor Farm Llc based in Lander, WY grazes up to 395 cattle between May and September
  • Woolery Ranch, Inc (L Hateley Lease) based in Kinnear, WY grazes up to 32 cattle between May and October and
  • Woolery Ranch, Inc based in Kinnear, WY grazes up to 365 cattle between May and October and
  • Tom Abernathy, Mildred Abernathy & William R Flagg based in Lander, WY grazes up to 70 cattle between May and September

The Pacific Creek allotment spans 195,387 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bar X Sheep Company based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 1,119 sheep in May and
  • Blair And Hay Land & Livestock Company based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 1,309 cattle between May and December
  • Applequist Family Trust (Delambert Lse) based in Farson, WY grazes up to 75 cattle in May
  • G&E Livestock Company based in Farson, WY grazes up to 2,835 sheep in December
  • Little Prospect Grazing Company, Llc based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 220 sheep in October
  • Hellyer Limited Partnership based in Lander, WY grazes up to 750 sheep in July

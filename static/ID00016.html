The Goodrich allotment spans 5,140 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Stippich Ranch based in Indian Valley, ID grazes up to 48 cattle between May and June
  • Paul & Kathryn Hughes based in Dayton, OH grazes up to 16 cattle between May and June
  • Gary & Carole Gallant based in Council, ID grazes up to 100 cattle between May and June and 100 cattle between October and November
  • C H E Enterprise based in Cambridge, ID grazes up to 50 cattle between May and June
  • Gary And Carole Gallant based in Council, ID grazes up to 105 cattle between May and June and 105 cattle between October and November
  • Heath And Aaron Chandler based in Monett, MO grazes up to 105 cattle between May and June and 105 cattle between October and November

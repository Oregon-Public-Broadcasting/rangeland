ARIZONA

The Whiterock-Soapstone allotment spans 18,284 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Leon Brinkerhoff based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 88 cattle between March and May and 88 cattle between October and February
  • Gubler Ranch, Llc based in Santa Clara, UT grazes up to
    • 264 cattle between August and October

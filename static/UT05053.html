The Rosebud allotment spans 20,861 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Arimo Corp. & C Bar Cattle Co. based in North Salt Lake City, UT grazes up to 5 cattle between October and January
  • Kunzler Ranch based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 111 cattle between October and January
  • Ingham Springs Llc based in West Jordan, UT grazes up to 345 cattle in May

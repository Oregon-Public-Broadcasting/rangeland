The Lake Channel allotment spans 18,728 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Kress, Lee & Judy based in American Falls, ID grazes up to 165 cattle between April and October
  • Kay Hunt based in American Falls, ID grazes up to 205 cattle between April and October
  • Kress, Art And Sons, Inc. based in American Falls, ID grazes up to 240 cattle between April and October
  • Lake Channel Ranch Inc based in American Falls, ID grazes up to 51 cattle between April and October
  • Kevin Ramsey based in American Falls, ID grazes up to 84 cattle between April and October

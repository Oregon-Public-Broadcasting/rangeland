The Mortensen-Holyoak allotment spans 15,633 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Esplin, Charles H. & Sons Land & Livestock, Inc. based in Salt Lake City, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between October and February,
  • Stowell, Coy Dba H.A. Farms, Inc. based in Parowan, UT grazes up to 279 sheep between November and March
  • Stubbs, Scott A. based in Parowan, UT grazes up to 868 sheep between November and February
  • Orton, Glenna H. based in Parowan, UT grazes up to 27 cattle between November and March, 27 cattle between June and October and 421 sheep between November and March

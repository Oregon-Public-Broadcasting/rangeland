CALIFORNIA

The Selic-Alaska allotment spans 4,982 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Estill Ranches Llc based in Gerlach, NV grazes up to
    • 1,075 sheep between July and September and 1,075 sheep in June
  • Washoe Livestock Water Company Llc based in Gerlach, NV grazes up to 1,000 sheep in July
  • Estill Ranches Llc. Ls Stockton Family Trust based in Gerlach, NV grazes up to 1,000 sheep between July and August

NEVADA

The Bottle Creek allotment spans 132,485 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Delong Ranches, Inc. based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to
    • 100 cattle between September and November
  • Wilson Ranch, Inc. based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to
    • 208 cattle between April and December
  • Mel Hummel based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to
    • 231 cattle between November and January and 175 cattle between June and August
  • Robert & Susan Hoenck based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 100 cattle between April and June

The Dry Canyon allotment spans 7,440 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Blanthorn, Sam & Tawni based in Grouse Creek, UT grazes up to 3 horses between May and October, 107 cattle between May and October
  • Simplot Land & Livestock Division based in Grand View, ID grazes up to 104 cattle between May and October
  • Grouse Creek Livestock Association based in Grouse Creek, UT grazes up to 28 cattle between May and October

The Reynolds Creek allotment spans 44,338 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Junayo Ranch Limited Partnership based in Boise, ID grazes up to 128 cattle between October and February, 442 cattle in March and 642 cattle between April and June
  • Bill Watterson based in Homedale, ID grazes up to 36 cattle between March and August
  • Richards, Tony & Brenda based in Murphy, ID grazes up to
    • 168 cattle between October and December and 303 cattle between April and August
  • Daniel & Bailey Richards based in Wilson, ID grazes up to
    • 20 cattle between April and August

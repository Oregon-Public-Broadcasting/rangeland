COLORADO

The Smith-Crawford allotment spans 15,045 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • David Smith Ranches Inc based in Meeker, CO grazes up to 110 cattle between May and October, 102 cattle between July and September and 40 cattle between May and June
  • Gayle R. Crawford based in Meeker, CO grazes up to 40 cattle between July and August, 20 cattle between November and July, 110 cattle between July and September, 50 cattle in October and 90 cattle between May and November

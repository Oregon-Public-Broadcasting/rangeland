OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Sutton Mountain allotment spans 25,315 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Fitzgerald Joe And Doris based in Mitchell, OR grazes up to 27 cattle between April and December
  • Misener John based in Keizer, OR grazes up to 5 cattle between April and December
  • Twin Buttes Llc based in Prineville, OR grazes up to 17 cattle between April and December

The Wood Hollow allotment spans 15,350 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Seth & Tate Weber based in Clawson, UT grazes up to 60 cattle between November and February and 44 cattle between March and June

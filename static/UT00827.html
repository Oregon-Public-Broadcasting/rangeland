The Kingston Canyon allotment spans 2,323 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mitchell R. Oliveira based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 25 cattle between November and January
  • Garrett Gleave based in Kingston, UT grazes up to 320 sheep in June and 320 sheep in October
  • Glen Allen based in Junction, UT grazes up to 320 sheep in June and 320 sheep in October

The Soaphole Com allotment spans 8,219 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Price Cattle Ranch, Llc based in Daniel, WY grazes up to 15 horses between May and October, 93 cattle between May and July,
  • Rimfire Ranch Llc based in Daniel, WY grazes up to 363 cattle between May and June
  • Sommers Ranch Llc based in Pinedale, WY grazes up to 16 horses between May and October, 185 cattle between May and June,

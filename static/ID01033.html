The Hammett #1 allotment spans 21,565 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Walker, John & Cindy based in King Hill, ID grazes up to 134 cattle between April and July
  • Iron Horse Ranch Llc based in Glenns Ferry, ID grazes up to
    • 906 cattle between October and November and 609 cattle between April and June
  • Casa Del Norte Lp based in Glenns Ferry, ID grazes up to 32 cattle between April and July

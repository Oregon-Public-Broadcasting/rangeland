The Warm Creek allotment spans 5,990 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Egan Land And Cattle Llc based in Dubois, ID grazes up to 29 cattle in October ,
  • Howard Taylor & Sons, Inc. based in Idaho Falls, ID grazes up to 612 cattle between October and January
  • Hoggan Brothers Inc. based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 9 horses in October , 15 cattle between October and January
  • Argora Ranch Inc based in Dubois, ID grazes up to 70 cattle in October

NEW MEXICO

The Lost Valley Community allotment spans 11,513 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Maestas, Gabriel, Christina & Son based in Albuquerque, NM grazes up to 119 cattle between March and May and 119 cattle between November and February
  • Dominguez, Charles based in Albuquerque, NM grazes up to 9 cattle between March and February
  • Sandoval, Restie & Paul Madrid based in Bernalillo, NM grazes up to 117 cattle between March and February

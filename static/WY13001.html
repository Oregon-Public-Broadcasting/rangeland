The White Acorn allotment spans 41,722 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Blair And Hay Land & Livestock Company based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 120 sheep between March and May,
  • G&E Livestock Company based in Farson, WY grazes up to 1,125 sheep in July and
  • Little Prospect Grazing Company, Llc based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 2,785 sheep in May ,
  • White Acorn Ranch, Inc based in Newton, MA grazes up to 601 cattle between May and October
  • Hellyer Limited Partnership based in Lander, WY grazes up to 3,600 sheep in July ,

The Doyleville Common allotment spans 3,653 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Lee Taramarcaz based in Parlin, CO grazes up to 56 cattle between May and June
  • Peterson Ranch, Inc. based in Gunnison, CO grazes up to
    • 60 cattle between May and June
  • Deb Rudibaugh based in Parlin, CO grazes up to 99 cattle between May and June

OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Louse Canyon Comm allotment spans 131,663 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Owyhee Grazing Association, Llc based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 812 cattle between June and September,
  • Gene E. Watson based in Lake Havasu City, AZ grazes up to 20 horses between May and September, 22 cattle between November and February, 607 cattle between May and July and 150 cattle between March and May
  • Kimble Wilkinson Ranches based in Mcdermitt, NV grazes up to 20 horses between March and October, 778 cattle between March and October and 39 cattle between October and February

The Dripping Spring allotment spans 16,981 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, all six were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Hans G. Hansen based in Elmo, UT grazes up to 60 cattle between March and May
  • Hansen, Kirk D. based in Cleveland, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between March and May
  • Hansen, Glen based in Cleveland, UT grazes up to 52 cattle between March and May
  • Gordon, Jesse And Brooks based in Elmo, UT grazes up to 52 cattle between March and May
  • Mcmullin, Lee D., Trust based in Cleveland, UT grazes up to 23 cattle between March and May
  • Jensen, Glen E. based in Elmo, UT grazes up to 124 cattle between March and May

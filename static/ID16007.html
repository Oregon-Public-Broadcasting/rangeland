The Edie Creek allotment spans 16,610 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Egan Land And Cattle Llc based in Dubois, ID grazes up to 120 cattle between May and August,
  • Howard Taylor & Sons, Inc. based in Idaho Falls, ID grazes up to 710 cattle between May and August and
  • Hoggan Brothers Inc. based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 20 horses between May and August, 125 cattle between May and August and
  • Argora Ranch Inc based in Dubois, ID grazes up to 281 cattle between May and August and

The Minersville No. 5 allotment spans 21,252 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Craw, Dale & Ada Richelle based in Minersville, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between May and October
  • Craw, Jim & Eileen based in Minersville, UT grazes up to 51 cattle between April and October
  • Pearson Ranch based in Minersville, UT grazes up to 174 cattle between June and October, 212 cattle between May and October and 100 cattle in April

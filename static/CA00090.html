CALIFORNIA

The Wagy Flat allotment spans 10,138 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

One ranch uses this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • George M. Prince Trust based in Bakersfield, CA grazes up to 146 cattle in February and 146 cattle between March and April
  • Rod Dowers based in Dyer, NV grazes up to 101 cattle between April and September

The Alder Creek allotment spans 6,320 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Pehrson, Randy And Peggy based in Moore, ID grazes up to 367 cattle between May and June
  • James C. Lambert based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 30 cattle between May and June
  • Alder Creek Ranch, Llc based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 45 cattle between May and July
  • Shane Rosenkrance based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 120 cattle between May and June

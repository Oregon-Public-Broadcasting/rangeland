The Stewart Creek allotment spans 165,025 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bartlett, Jess & Debbi based in Bairoil, WY grazes up to 89 cattle between November and February and 89 cattle between March and April
  • Stewart Creek Llc based in Alcova, WY grazes up to 760 cattle between May and December
  • Richard Carter based in Ten Sleep, WY grazes up to 505 cattle between May and November

OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Camp Creek Community allotment spans 17,861 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Sigman Ranch Llc based in Prineville, OR grazes up to 52 cattle between April and May
  • Oregon Ranching Co based in Los Angeles, CA grazes up to 182 cattle between April and September

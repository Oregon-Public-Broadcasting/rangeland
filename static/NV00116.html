NEVADA

The Pumpernickel allotment spans 126,142 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Chester F. Dawson Exemption Trust based in Las Vegas, NV grazes up to 58 cattle between March and September and 58 cattle between December and February
  • Richard & Nancy Rosasco based in Golconda, NV grazes up to 212 cattle between November and May
  • Roger Johnson based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 120 cattle between October and February and 120 cattle between March and May
  • Ellc Grazing Membership, Llc based in Battle Mountain, NV grazes up to 3,788 sheep between March and June and 3,788 sheep between October and February

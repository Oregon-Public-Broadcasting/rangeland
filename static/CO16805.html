COLORADO

The Hawxhurst Common allotment spans 3,816 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Hittle Cattle Company based in Collbran, CO grazes up to 249 cattle between May and June
  • Les Hittle based in Collbran, CO grazes up to 59 cattle between May and July
  • Mitchell, Don & Joan based in Collbran, CO grazes up to 36 cattle between May and July

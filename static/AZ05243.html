ARIZONA

The White Pockets allotment spans 3,450 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Paria Valley Livestock Llc based in Page, AZ grazes up to 40 cattle between March and February
  • Reeve, Ashby, Iva, Bernice & John based in Hurricane, UT grazes up to 3 horses between March and February, 12 cattle between March and May and 12 cattle between January and February
  • Bernice Reeve based in Hurricane, UT grazes up to 3 horses between March and July, 3 horses between August and February, 12 cattle between January and February and 12 cattle between March and May

The Horse Flat allotment spans 4,203 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Ford Ranch Inc based in Cambridge, ID grazes up to
    • 160 cattle in November and 160 cattle between April and June
  • Charles R. Edwards based in Cambridge, ID grazes up to 95 cattle between April and June and 95 cattle in November
  • Randy Noah based in Cambridge, ID grazes up to 113 cattle between April and May
  • Keith And Karen Hood based in Cambridge, ID grazes up to 11 cattle between April and June and 47 cattle in November

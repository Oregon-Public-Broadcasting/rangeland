Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

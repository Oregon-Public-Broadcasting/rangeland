The Fossil Butte allotment spans 39,964 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Nick Nettleton based in Glenns Ferry, ID grazes up to 76 cattle between October and February
  • Joyce Livestock Co. based in Murphy, ID grazes up to 24 horses between October and February, 189 cattle between October and February
  • Vernon H. & Kenneth Kershner based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 51 cattle between October and February

NEVADA

The Desert Valley allotment spans 56,965 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Delong Ranches, Inc. based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 29 cattle in December , 70 cattle in September and 70 cattle between October and November
  • Mel Hummel based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 286 cattle between April and August, 121 cattle in August , 39 cattle in September and 27 cattle between October and November

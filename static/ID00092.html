The East Garden Valley allotment spans 2,352 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Don K Weilmunster based in Garden Valley, ID grazes up to 26 cattle between May and July
  • Stack Rock Grazing Association based in Nampa, ID grazes up to 518 sheep between May and November
  • Terry Day Ee Trust based in Boise, ID grazes up to 22 cattle between June and September
  • Cole Youren based in Garden Valley, ID grazes up to 30 cattle between May and July

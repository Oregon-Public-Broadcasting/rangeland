The Peters allotment spans 1,213 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Alex N. Kunkel based in Twin Falls, ID grazes up to 19 cattle between May and September
  • Peters, John L. And Teri Lyn based in Filer, ID grazes up to 76 cattle between May and September
  • Deep Creek Ranch Llc based in Ashton, ID grazes up to 35 cattle between May and June
  • Josh And Delea Williamson based in Twin Falls, ID grazes up to 30 cattle between May and June

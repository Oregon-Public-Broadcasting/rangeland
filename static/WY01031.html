The Himes Group allotment spans 18,989 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Gifford Ranch Llc based in Basin, WY grazes up to 155 cattle between September and November,
  • Schatz Irrevocable Living Trust based in Lovell, WY grazes up to 45 cattle between May and July and 45 cattle in October
  • Kenneth & Dee Dee Mcnabb based in Greybull, WY grazes up to 37 cattle between April and June

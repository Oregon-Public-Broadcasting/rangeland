The Red Hills allotment spans 24,576 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Lone Pine Bc Ranch, Llc based in Moses Lake,, WA grazes up to 131 cattle between May and October
  • Berend Friehe based in Moses Lake, WA grazes up to 166 cattle between May and October
  • Scott L. Whitworth based in May, ID grazes up to 297 cattle between May and October

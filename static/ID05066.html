The West Ridge allotment spans 1,240 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jamie M. Hillman based in Rexburg, ID grazes up to 200 cattle between October and December
  • Nick Hillman based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 407 cattle between October and December
  • Randy Hillman based in Rexburg, ID grazes up to 233 cattle between October and December
  • Ron Hillman based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 60 cattle between October and December

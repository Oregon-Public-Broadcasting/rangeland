The Tower Creek allotment spans 4,409 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Fourth Of July Creek, L.L.C. based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 65 cattle between May and August
  • Jay And Chyenne Smith based in Carmen, ID grazes up to 51 cattle between May and August
  • Bill Bernt based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 4 horses between April and September and 4 horses between September and December
  • Thomas L. Mcfarland based in Carmen, ID grazes up to 24 cattle between May and June
  • Clarice Hokanson based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 10 horses between May and September

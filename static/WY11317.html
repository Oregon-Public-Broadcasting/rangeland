The Bridger Airport allotment spans 20,757 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Larson Livestock Inc based in Lyman, WY grazes up to
    • 4,240 sheep between May and July and 500 sheep between September and November
  • Micheli Herford Ranch (Goodrick Lse) based in Fort Bridger, WY grazes up to 21 cattle between May and October
  • Giorgis Family Trust based in Fort Bridger, WY grazes up to 14 cattle between May and September and 47 cattle between May and October
  • Aimone, Brenda (Goodrick Lse) based in Fort Bridger, WY grazes up to 21 cattle between May and October
  • Fraughton, Wendell K based in Mountain View, WY grazes up to 71 cattle between July and September, 100 cattle in May and 200 cattle between May and June

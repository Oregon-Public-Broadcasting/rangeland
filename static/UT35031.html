UTAH

The Iron Wash allotment spans 130,510 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Murdock Livestock based in Mapleton, UT grazes up to 232 cattle between November and April
  • Steven G. Smith based in Lehi, UT grazes up to 302 cattle between November and April
  • Johnson Mountain Ranch based in Aurora, UT grazes up to 302 cattle between November and April

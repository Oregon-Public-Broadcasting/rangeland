OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Timber Canyon allotment spans 5,622 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jack Corning based in Richland, OR grazes up to 38 cattle between April and May
  • Hammond Family Trust Lease based in Richland, OR grazes up to 180 cattle between April and May and 88 cattle in November
  • William M. Stephenson based in Richland, OR grazes up to 22 cattle between April and May and 22 cattle in November
  • Gordon Bond based in Richland, OR grazes up to 36 cattle between April and May

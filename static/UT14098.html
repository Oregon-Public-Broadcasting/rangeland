The Gordon Point allotment spans 329 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Danny Bulloch based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 2 cattle between June and September
  • D. Tyree Bulloch based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 4 cattle between June and September
  • Bulloch, Boyd based in Caliente, NV grazes up to 6 cattle between June and August
  • Shawn Bulloch based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 1 cattle between June and July

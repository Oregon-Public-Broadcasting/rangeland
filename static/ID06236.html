The Yearian Creek allotment spans 24,878 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Von Bean based in Lemhi, ID grazes up to 132 cattle between May and September
  • Shiner Ranch Inc based in Lemhi, ID grazes up to
    • 32 cattle between May and September 5 horses between May and November and 293 cattle between May and September
  • Riata Livestock based in Lemhi, ID grazes up to 197 cattle between May and September

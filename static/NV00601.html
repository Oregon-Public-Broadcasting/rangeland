NEVADA

The Railroad Pass allotment spans 27,025 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • The Little Paris Sheep Company, Llc based in Jiggs, NV grazes up to 467 sheep between April and November, 365 sheep between April and November,
  • Harold Rother Farms, Inc based in Spring Creek, NV grazes up to 176 cattle between May and October
  • Pete Goicoechea based in Eureka, NV grazes up to 50 cattle between May and October,

The Road Canyon allotment spans 4,695 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Fiscus, Theron & Laurie based in Carey, ID grazes up to 49 cattle between May and June
  • Ray Baird based in Carey, ID grazes up to
    • 79 cattle between May and June and 36 cattle between October and November
  • Thomas & Brenda Peck based in Carey, ID grazes up to
    • 75 cattle between May and June and 53 cattle between October and November
  • W. D. Pardue based in Carey, ID grazes up to 170 cattle between May and June and 73 cattle between October and November

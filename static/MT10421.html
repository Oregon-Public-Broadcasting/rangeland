MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Strawberry Ridge allotment spans 2,020 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Pony Ranch based in Manhattan, MT grazes up to 92 cattle between July and September
  • Ohs, Gerald based in Pony, MT grazes up to 8 cattle between July and September
  • Ohs Karl Ranch based in Harrison, MT grazes up to 160 cattle between July and September

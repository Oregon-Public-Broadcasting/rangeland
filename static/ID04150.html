The Gray's Lake allotment spans 1,412 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bob Sollis based in Shelley, ID grazes up to 450 cattle in October and 450 cattle in May
  • Schweider, Boyd & Jack based in Idaho Falls, ID grazes up to 200 cattle in May
  • Marianne Snarr based in Rigby, ID grazes up to 225 cattle in May and 225 cattle in October
  • Ted A Thompson based in Shelley, ID grazes up to 100 cattle in October and 100 cattle in May

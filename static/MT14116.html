MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Hawk Coulee allotment spans 5,358 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Matt Miller based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 106 cattle between June and November
  • Lee, Lynn & Clay Cornwell based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 8 cattle between March and February
  • Mary Nyquist based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 2 cattle between April and November
  • Anna Nyquist Ranch, Llp based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 1 cattle between April and November
  • Jeffrey Nyquist based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 6 cattle between March and February

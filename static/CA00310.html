CALIFORNIA

The Tule Mountain allotment spans 49,376 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Green Valley Enterprises based in Alturas, CA grazes up to 671 cattle between May and September
  • Likely Land And Livestock Company based in Likely, CA grazes up to 757 cattle between May and September
  • Espil Sheep Co. based in Sparks, NV grazes up to 583 sheep between July and September
  • Mcgarva Ranch - Range Division based in Likely, CA grazes up to 179 cattle between May and September

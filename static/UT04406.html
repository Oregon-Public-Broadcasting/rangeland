The Oak City allotment spans 11,722 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Eight ranches use this land. Of the eight permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Around Back Ranch based in Leamington, UT grazes up to 70 cattle between November and March and
  • Jim & Irene Lamont based in South Jordan, UT grazes up to 102 cattle between November and May
  • Sinks Land Company based in Oak City, UT grazes up to 25 cattle between November and May
  • Joseph T Finlinson Inc based in Leamington, UT grazes up to 36 cattle between November and March and
  • Jay Burton Lovell Family Trust based in Oak City, UT grazes up to 66 cattle between November and May
  • Monte C. Nielson based in Oak City, UT grazes up to 48 cattle between November and May
  • Vance Finlinson based in Oak City, UT grazes up to 92 cattle between November and May
  • Olan J & Kara Marie Finlinson based in Delta, UT grazes up to 36 cattle between November and May

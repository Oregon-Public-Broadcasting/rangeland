The Harkness/Beach Hollo allotment spans 3,240 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

    • 1,044 sheep between August and September and 1,044 sheep between May and June
  • Dimick, Dan N Or Canda L based in Lava Hot Springs, ID grazes up to 8 cattle in May
  • Andrus, Kenneth And Jim based in Lava Hot Springs, ID grazes up to 22 cattle in May and 22 cattle in November

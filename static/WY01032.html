The Lovell Group 1 allotment spans 10,436 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Gifford Ranch Llc based in Basin, WY grazes up to 136 cattle in July , 136 cattle in October ,
  • Cottonwood Canyon Cattle Company, Llc based in Granby, CO grazes up to 47 cattle in October and 48 cattle in June
  • Tippetts Farms, Llc. based in Lovell, WY grazes up to 75 cattle between May and July and 97 cattle between October and November
  • Larry And Spencer Ellis based in Lovell, WY grazes up to 100 cattle in October

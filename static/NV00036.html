NEVADA

The Little Owyhee allotment spans 560,815 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Nfc Land & Cattle based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 254 cattle between November and February, 1,000 cattle in June , 2,500 cattle in June , 3,500 cattle between March and May, 1,000 cattle between July and August and 168 cattle between September and February
  • Jerry Harper based in Paradise Valley, NV grazes up to 103 cattle between October and February, 600 cattle between July and August, 630 cattle in April , 350 cattle in March , 450 cattle in March and 665 cattle between May and June

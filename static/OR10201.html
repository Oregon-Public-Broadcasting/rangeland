OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Allotment 2 allotment spans 48,569 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Maynard Alves Land & Livestock, Westfall, Llc based in Westfall, OR grazes up to 45 cattle between April and October and 2 cattle between April and October
  • William & Cindy Romans based in Harper, OR grazes up to 600 cattle between April and October
  • J.R. Land & Livestock based in Westfall, OR grazes up to 438 cattle between April and October and 11 cattle between April and October
  • Calvin V. Haueter based in Harper, OR grazes up to 29 cattle between April and October

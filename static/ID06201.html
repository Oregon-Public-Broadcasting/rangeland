The Haynes Creek allotment spans 7,157 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mulkey Ranch based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 93 cattle between May and September
  • Jack Jakovac based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 88 cattle between May and September
  • Snook Ranches based in Salmon, ID grazes up to 172 cattle between May and September
  • Dennis And Miriam Schlabach based in St. Ignatius, MT grazes up to 87 cattle between May and September

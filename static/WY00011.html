The Joe Henry allotment spans 7,000 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Lazy Bighorn Ranch Llc based in Covington, VA grazes up to 40 cattle between November and December, 50 cattle between April and June, 400 sheep between April and June and 400 sheep between November and December
  • Breeden Brothers based in Ten Sleep, WY grazes up to 195 cattle between November and December, 230 cattle between May and June and 200 cattle in April
  • Greet Ranch Inc based in Ten Sleep, WY grazes up to 77 cattle between November and December and 74 cattle between April and June

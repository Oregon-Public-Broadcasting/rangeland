COLORADO

The Whitewater Common allotment spans 22,596 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Massey, Oscar T. Massey, Janice M. based in Whitewater, CO grazes up to 64 cattle between December and January, 88 cattle between April and May,
  • Lumbardy, Donald Et Al based in Whitewater, CO grazes up to 6 cattle between September and December and 6 cattle between April and June
  • Howard And Janie Van Winkle based in Fruita, CO grazes up to 208 cattle between April and June and 202 cattle between December and January

MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Williams Basin allotment spans 10,272 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Carlson, Gordon And Annette based in Belfry, MT grazes up to 25 cattle between December and February, 87 cattle between May and August and 25 cattle in March
  • Jackson, Richard R. based in Belfry, MT grazes up to 33 cattle between May and October
  • Hardiman, Stephen & Sonya based in Belfry, MT grazes up to 44 cattle between May and October

The South Labarge Common allotment spans 97,965 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the seven permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Calvin Barnes Family Limited Partnership based in Green River, WY grazes up to 371 cattle between May and September
  • Fox Ranch At Spring Creek, Llc based in La Barge, WY grazes up to 353 cattle between May and June
  • Okelberry, Ray & Brian based in Goshen, UT grazes up to 309 cattle between May and June and 87 cattle between July and October
  • V Cross Cattle Co. based in Kemmerer, WY grazes up to 650 cattle between May and June and 162 cattle between June and October
  • Jones, Bruce E based in Kemmerer, WY grazes up to 464 cattle between May and October
  • Diamond H Ranch, Inc based in Kemmerer, WY grazes up to 3 horses between May and October, 373 cattle between May and October
  • Fish Creek Flying W Ranches, Inc. based in Big Piney, WY grazes up to 536 cattle between June and October

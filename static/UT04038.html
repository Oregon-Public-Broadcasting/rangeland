The Stansbury Island Nor allotment spans 2,300 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mark Bleazard based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 12 cattle between November and February and 12 cattle in March
  • Six Mile Ranch based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 10 horses between November and May, 142 cattle between November and March
  • Mike C. Worthington based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 10 cattle between November and March

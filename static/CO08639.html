COLORADO

The Upper Cottonwood allotment spans 1,125 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

    • 19 cattle between May and June, 2 cattle between June and September,
  • Schlegel Cattle Company, Llc based in Burns, CO grazes up to 19 cattle between May and June, 3 cattle between June and September,
  • Smith, Maynard D. based in Eagle, CO grazes up to 28 yearling cattle between May and June, 28 cattle between May and June

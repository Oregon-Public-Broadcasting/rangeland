The Deeth allotment spans 125,397 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Cross Ranch Grazing Llc based in Deeth, NV grazes up to 4 cattle between March and February, 355 cattle between March and May, 225 cattle between April and December, 375 cattle between April and December, 355 cattle between August and February, 138 cattle between April and December, 259 cattle between April and December, 242 cattle between April and December and 370 cattle between April and December
  • Bilbao Ranch Llc based in Shoshone, ID grazes up to 3 cattle between March and February, 117 cattle between April and December and 208 cattle between April and December

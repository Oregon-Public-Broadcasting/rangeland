The Boone Creek allotment spans 9,826 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Boone Creek Partnership based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 25 cattle in November , 25 cattle in October and 25 cattle between May and June
  • Challis Creek Cattle Company, Partnership based in Challis, ID grazes up to 937 cattle in November , 937 cattle between May and June and 937 cattle in October
  • Johnson, Joan Et Al based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 10 horses between May and November
  • Churndasher Ranch based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 247 cattle in October , 247 cattle in November and 247 cattle between May and June

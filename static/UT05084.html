The Fisher Creek allotment spans 3,520 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, all six were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Spackman Ranch, Llc based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 18 cattle between August and September, 31 cattle between May and July and
  • Westmoreland Ranch based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 21 cattle between May and September
  • Kunzler Ranch based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 6 cattle between May and September and
  • Royce Larsen based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 65 cattle in September
  • Robert F. Montgomery based in North Ogden, UT grazes up to 12 cattle between May and September
  • Bar C Land & Livestock Llc based in Park Valley, UT grazes up to 4 cattle between May and September

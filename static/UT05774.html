The Squidike allotment spans 36,116 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the seven permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Shane Stewart based in Meadow, UT grazes up to 37 cattle between June and October
  • Brandon George based in Kanosh, UT grazes up to 138 cattle between November and March
  • Tom & Karl Lund based in Ephraim, UT grazes up to 297 sheep in March and 332 sheep between November and March
  • Ray Stewart based in Meadow, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between June and October
  • L B Ranch based in Meadow, UT grazes up to 20 cattle between June and October
  • Leo D. Stott based in Meadow, UT grazes up to 42 cattle between June and October
  • Van Stott based in Meadow, UT grazes up to 65 cattle between June and October

MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Lower Wayne Creek allotment spans 6,510 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Wing Road Farms based in Hogeland, MT grazes up to 300 cattle between May and December
  • Alex J. & Jessi R. Billmayer based in Hogeland, MT grazes up to 75 cattle between May and December
  • Casey H. Billmayer based in Hogeland, MT grazes up to 75 cattle between May and December

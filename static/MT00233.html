MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The Devils Creek Common allotment spans 4,260 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Pierson, Kelly And Jodi based in Brusett, MT grazes up to 26 yearling cattle between May and August
  • Phipps Ranch, Inc. based in Brusett, MT grazes up to 25 yearling cattle between May and August, 24 cattle between May and August
  • Gidlewski, James And Cynthia based in Brusett, MT grazes up to 20 yearling cattle between May and August, 20 cattle between May and August
  • Table Top Ranch based in Brusett, MT grazes up to 47 cattle between May and August

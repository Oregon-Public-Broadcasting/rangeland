NEVADA

The Fish Creek Ranch allotment spans 287,984 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Wildhorse Ranching Co. Llc based in Hazen, NV grazes up to
    • 600 cattle in March and 600 cattle between April and May
  • Borba Land And Cattle Llc based in Eureka, NV grazes up to 1 cattle in March and 506 cattle between November and March
  • Segura Ranch Holdings Llc based in Cedar City, UT grazes up to 797 sheep between June and October

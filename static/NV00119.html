NEVADA

The Rawhide allotment spans 122,300 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • John Olagaray based in Lodi, CA grazes up to 1,300 sheep in April and
  • The Shining K, Llc based in Fernley, NV grazes up to 214 cattle between January and October
  • Vesco Ranch based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to 30 cattle between April and November and
  • Mick Casey based in Fallon, NV grazes up to 40 cattle between April and December

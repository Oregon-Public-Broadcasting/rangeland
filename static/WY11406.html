The Little Creek allotment spans 4,937 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Broadbent, Jr Grazing Association, Llc based in Salt Lake City, UT grazes up to
    • 59 cattle between May and October and 20 cattle between May and October
  • Guild Ranch based in Fort Bridger, WY grazes up to 29 cattle between May and October
  • Byrne, Bonnie J Or William C based in Mountain View, WY grazes up to 28 cattle between June and August

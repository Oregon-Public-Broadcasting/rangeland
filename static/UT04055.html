The Mercur Can-W. Ophir allotment spans 19,972 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Monroe, Dix And Georgia based in Ophir, UT grazes up to 29 cattle between March and May and 30 cattle between November and December
  • Howard Ault based in American Fork, UT grazes up to 785 sheep between March and May and 785 sheep between November and February
  • Justin Lyon (Ault Lease) based in Cedar Fort, UT grazes up to 403 sheep between November and May
  • Gillmor Ranching Llc (Ault Lease) based in Bountiful, UT grazes up to 385 sheep between November and May

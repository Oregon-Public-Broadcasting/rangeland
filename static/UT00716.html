The Hector Hollow allotment spans 1,955 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jeffery Ranches Inc. based in Fremont, UT grazes up to 41 cattle in May
  • Taylor, Philip And Audrey based in Bicknell, UT grazes up to 99 sheep between November and February,
  • Ned H. Taylor based in Fremont, UT grazes up to 37 cattle in May

The Nine Mile Knoll allotment spans 11,222 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Nick Hillman based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 603 cattle between May and June,
  • Randy Hillman based in Rexburg, ID grazes up to 233 cattle between May and June,
  • Ron Hillman based in Hamer, ID grazes up to 60 cattle between May and June,

COLORADO

The Tracy Common allotment spans 14,513 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Gery J. Dirks based in Saguache, CO grazes up to 90 cattle between June and October
  • Danny, Denise Vivian & Delbert Temple based in Center, CO grazes up to 90 cattle between June and October
  • Davey Family Partnership, Ltd. based in Saguache, CO grazes up to 90 cattle between June and October

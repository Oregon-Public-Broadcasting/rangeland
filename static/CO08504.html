COLORADO

The W Hardscrabble Com allotment spans 16,277 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Wilson Ranch, Llc based in Evergreen, CO grazes up to 128 cattle between May and June
  • Gypsum Creek Ranch based in Gypsum, CO grazes up to 100 cattle between May and June
  • Gerard Brothers Partnership, Lllp based in Gypsum, CO grazes up to 395 cattle between May and June

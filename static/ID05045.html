The Clear Creek allotment spans 10,240 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Douglas H. Ward based in Almo, ID grazes up to 100 cattle in April , 25 cattle in December ,
  • Roscoe B. Ward, Joyce Ward & Trudy Pace based in Malta, ID grazes up to 36 cattle in April , 45 cattle in December ,
  • Spencer Brothers based in Malta, ID grazes up to 110 cattle between November and December and 109 cattle in April
  • Big B Ranch based in Malta, ID grazes up to 59 cattle between November and December
  • Round Mountain Ranch based in Malta, ID grazes up to 40 cattle in June

WYOMING

The South Desert Allot. allotment spans 34,563 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Rees-Triangle 5, Llc based in Big Piney, WY grazes up to 508 cattle between May and June
  • The 67 Family Limited Partnership based in Big Piney, WY grazes up to 247 cattle between May and June
  • Tolton, Tim & Taran based in Big Piney, WY grazes up to 250 cattle between May and June
  • C And D Enterprises, Llc based in Ogden, UT grazes up to 148 cattle between May and June
  • Miller L. & L. Co. based in Big Piney, WY grazes up to 228 cattle between May and June

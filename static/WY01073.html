WYOMING

The Sage Creek Group allotment spans 12,218 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Rafter Reverse Four Ranch Llc based in Cody, WY grazes up to 47 cattle between June and October
  • Bell Land & Cattle Company based in Cody, WY grazes up to 35 cattle between June and October
  • Jones Trust based in Powell, WY grazes up to 210 cattle between June and October

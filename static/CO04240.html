COLORADO

The Alta Lake allotment spans 5,192 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Carlos Garcia based in Antonito, CO grazes up to 48 cattle between November and December and 48 cattle between April and June
  • Reginaldo G. Garcia based in Monte Vista, CO grazes up to 48 cattle between April and June and 48 cattle between November and December
  • Roberto Garcia based in Antonito, CO grazes up to 48 cattle between April and June and 48 cattle between November and December

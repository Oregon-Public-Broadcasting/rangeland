NEW MEXICO

The La Segita Community allotment spans 2,960 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Duran, Roberto F. & Duran, Lorenzo P. based in Antonito, CO grazes up to 27 cattle between October and November and 20 cattle between May and July
  • Jerry A. Duran based in Antonito, CO grazes up to 30 cattle between May and July and 40 cattle between October and November
  • Lipio Duran based in Antonito, CO grazes up to 35 cattle between May and July and 47 cattle between October and November

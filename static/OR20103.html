OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Poall Creek allotment spans 2,833 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • William And Nancy Moore based in Unity, OR grazes up to 63 cattle between September and December
  • Heid, William A. And Monte G. based in Vale, OR grazes up to 81 cattle between September and November
  • Brad Hevner based in Brogan, OR grazes up to 135 cattle between September and November

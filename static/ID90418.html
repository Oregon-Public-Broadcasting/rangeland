The Black Canyon allotment spans 52,927 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Nine ranches use this land. Of the nine permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Goodtime Association based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 1,015 sheep between April and May
  • L & T Farms Llc based in Fairfield, ID grazes up to 561 cattle between April and June and
  • Slash L Grazing Association based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 181 cattle between April and June
  • Earl V. Brown based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 52 cattle between April and August and 11 cattle between April and August
  • Sabala Farms Inc based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 87 horses between September and December
  • Eric Or Sarah Nielson based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 11 horses between April and August, 2 horses between April and August and
  • Fir Grove, Ltd based in King Hill, ID grazes up to 241 cattle between November and December
  • Toby Flick based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 18 cattle between April and August and 87 cattle between April and August
  • Patterson Land And Livestock Co based in Gooding, ID grazes up to 168 cattle between April and August

NEW MEXICO

The Esperanza Allot allotment spans 6,771 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Esperanza Grazing Association based in Amarillo, TX grazes up to 92 cattle between June and October
  • Esperanza Grazing Association based in Espanola, NM grazes up to 153 cattle between May and October
  • Chacon Family Llc based in Espanola, NM grazes up to 103 cattle between May and October

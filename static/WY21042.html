The Beaver Creek allotment spans 7,840 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Thoman, W & M Ranches, Llc based in Green River, WY grazes up to 300 cattle between June and September
  • Hoffman, Roy D Et Al based in Randolph, UT grazes up to 177 cattle between June and September
  • Schulthess, Wallace based in Woodruff, UT grazes up to 99 cattle between June and September
  • Hoffman,Roy based in Kemmerer, WY grazes up to 72 cattle between June and September

NEVADA

The Eagle Rock 1 allotment spans 8,345 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Johnston, Jim Or Johnston, Claudine based in Meridian, ID grazes up to 6 cattle between May and October,
  • Glaser Land And Livestock Co. based in Halleck, NV grazes up to 263 cattle between April and August,
  • Rabbit Creek Ranch, Llc. based in Reno, NV grazes up to 44 cattle between April and August

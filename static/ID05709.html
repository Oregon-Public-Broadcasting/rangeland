The Wildhorse allotment spans 24,642 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the seven permits issued for this allotment, six were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Walt And Debbie Johnson based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 113 cattle in October and 113 cattle between May and September
  • Whitworth, Jr. Herbert (Lease) based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 179 cattle in October and 179 cattle between May and September
  • Clinton & Ester Bitton Family Limited Partnership based in Pocatello, ID grazes up to 104 cattle in October and 104 cattle between May and September
  • Dickey Livestock based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 491 cattle in October and 491 cattle between May and September
  • D & L based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 55 cattle between May and September and 55 cattle in October
  • Whitworth, Jr. Herbert based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 768 cattle between May and September and 768 cattle in October
  • Williams, E Logan (Dusty) based in Mackay, ID grazes up to 4 horses between May and December, 136 cattle between May and September and 136 cattle in October

The Kimama allotment spans 27,785 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Lava Lake Land & Livestock Llc based in Hailey, ID grazes up to
    • 100 sheep between April and June, 62 sheep between October and December,
  • Etcheverry Sheep Co. based in Rupert, ID grazes up to
    • 80 sheep between October and December and 82 sheep between April and June
  • Flat Top Grazing Assn based in Carey, ID grazes up to
    • 377 cattle between April and June, 2,126 sheep between April and June, 505 cattle between October and December, 2,856 sheep between October and December,
  • Fish Creek Angus Llc based in Carey, ID grazes up to 367 sheep between October and December, 300 sheep between April and June,
  • Pine Mountain Grazing Association based in Carey, ID grazes up to 61 sheep between October and December,
  • Muldoon Grazing Assn based in Hailey, ID grazes up to 395 sheep between October and December, 400 sheep between April and June,

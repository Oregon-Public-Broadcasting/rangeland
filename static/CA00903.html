CALIFORNIA

The Home Camp allotment spans 142,434 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Betty Cockrell based in Cedarville, CA grazes up to 175 cattle in April , 230 cattle in September and 457 cattle between April and August
  • Grove Brothers based in Eagleville, CA grazes up to 685 cattle between April and August, 264 cattle in April and 343 cattle in September
  • Jim Cockrell based in Lake City, CA grazes up to 457 cattle between April and August, 175 cattle in April and 230 cattle in September
  • Robert L. Cockrell based in Cedarville, CA grazes up to 112 cattle in September , 86 cattle in April and 229 cattle between April and August

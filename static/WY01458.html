The Cumberland Flats allotment spans 11,806 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Julian Land & Lvst Corp based in Kemmerer, WY grazes up to 1,325 sheep between September and November and 1,585 sheep between May and July
  • Thoman, W & M Ranches, Llc based in Green River, WY grazes up to
    • 44 sheep between May and November
  • Bear River Land And Grazing Co. based in Salt Lake City, UT grazes up to 180 cattle between May and October
  • Hatch, Douglas And Joanna based in Randolph, UT grazes up to 43 cattle between May and July
  • Sims Livestock based in Evanston, WY grazes up to 1,287 sheep between September and November and 1,430 sheep between April and July

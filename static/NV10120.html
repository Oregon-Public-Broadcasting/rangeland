NEVADA

The South Coal Valley allotment spans 46,701 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • John Uhalde & Co based in Ely, NV grazes up to
    • 1,517 sheep between December and February and 1,517 sheep between March and April
  • Varlin S Higbee based in Alamo, NV grazes up to 18 cattle between March and May and 18 cattle between September and February
  • Vaughn M Higbee based in Alamo, NV grazes up to 67 cattle between September and February and 67 cattle between March and May
  • Higbee Brothers based in Alamo, NV grazes up to 14 cattle between September and February and 14 cattle between March and May

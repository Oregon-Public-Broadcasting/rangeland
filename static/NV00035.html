NEVADA

The William Stock allotment spans 63,989 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • T Five Ranch Llc based in Paradise Valley, NV grazes up to
    • 396 cattle between April and June and 1,210 cattle between March and June
  • Ninety Six Ranch based in Paradise Valley, NV grazes up to 163 cattle between April and June and 636 cattle between March and June

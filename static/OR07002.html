OREGON/WASHINGTON

The West Warm Springs allotment spans 297,375 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Chance L Peila based in Burns, OR grazes up to 154 cattle between April and September and 60 cattle in April
  • William S & Lori K Peila based in Hines, OR grazes up to 492 cattle between April and September and 244 cattle in April
  • J V Moon & Sons based in Hines, OR grazes up to 454 cattle between April and August, 216 cattle in April and 454 cattle between August and September
  • Ketscher Cattle Co based in Hines, OR grazes up to 1,005 cattle between April and September and 380 cattle in April

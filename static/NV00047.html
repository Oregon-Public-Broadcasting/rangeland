NEVADA

The Wilder-Quinn allotment spans 188,274 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Quinn River Crossing Ranch, Llc based in Lafayette, CA grazes up to 200 cattle between November and February, 1,460 cattle between November and December, 200 cattle between March and September, 1,460 cattle between March and August and 1,460 cattle in February
  • Casey, Pete & Naomi based in Fields, OR grazes up to 34 cattle between December and February
  • Dufurrena Sheep Company based in Winnemucca, NV grazes up to
    • 482 sheep between April and May, 482 sheep between June and September and 710 sheep in May

UTAH

The Salt Wash allotment spans 37,880 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the seven permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bryan T. & Julie L. Weber based in Clawson, UT grazes up to 17 cattle in June , 33 cattle between November and January and 33 cattle between March and May
  • Rainbow Glass Ranch based in Orangeville, UT grazes up to 38 cattle in June , 92 cattle between March and May and 92 cattle between November and January
  • Gordon, Earl & Sheila - Trust based in Huntington, UT grazes up to 94 cattle between November and June
  • San Rafael Ranch based in Ferron, UT grazes up to 129 cattle between November and January, 129 cattle between March and May and 119 cattle in June
  • Mcelprang, Lee based in Huntington, UT grazes up to 56 cattle in June , 93 cattle between November and January and 93 cattle between March and May
  • Riley, Mike Or April based in Ferron, UT grazes up to 50 cattle between March and May and 50 cattle between November and January
  • Mcelprang, Lee Or Leon based in Huntington, UT grazes up to 25 cattle between March and June and 25 cattle between November and January

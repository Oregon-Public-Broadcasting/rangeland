CALIFORNIA

The Corral allotment spans 1,560 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Betty Cockrell based in Cedarville, CA grazes up to 11 cattle between September and October,
  • Grove Brothers based in Eagleville, CA grazes up to 17 cattle between September and October,
  • Jim Cockrell based in Lake City, CA grazes up to 11 cattle between September and October,
  • Robert L. Cockrell based in Cedarville, CA grazes up to 6 cattle between September and October,

The Jump Creek allotment spans 8,683 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Chipmunk Grazing Association based in Marsing, ID grazes up to 116 cattle between July and September
    • 385 cattle between June and September
  • Jaca Livestock based in Nampa, ID grazes up to 188 cattle between July and September
  • Hook Family Llc based in Murphy, ID grazes up to 148 cattle between July and September

The Silver Creek allotment spans 2,750 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Hennefer, Dennis & Mary Ann based in Carey, ID grazes up to 26 cattle between May and July
  • John Flood based in Hailey, ID grazes up to 10 cattle between May and July
  • Hennefer Farms based in Carey, ID grazes up to 37 cattle between May and July

The Notch Butte allotment spans 10,878 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Oneida Farms, Inc. based in Jerome, ID grazes up to 151 cattle between April and September,
  • Harold Cook based in Dietrich, ID grazes up to 23 cattle between April and September
  • Valley View Of Magic Valley, Inc. based in Jerome, ID grazes up to 111 cattle between April and September
  • Rupert Jimmie D based in Jerome, ID grazes up to 50 cattle between April and September

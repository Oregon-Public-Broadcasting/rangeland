The Boulder Lake Com allotment spans 4,900 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • J. Thomas Johnston, Md & Jane H. Johnston, Ttees based in Pinedale, WY grazes up to 150 cattle between July and August,
  • Bousman Lvstk. Co. based in Boulder, WY grazes up to 33 cattle between May and June
  • Circle Nine Ranch based in Boulder, WY grazes up to 200 cattle between June and August
  • Rogers, Carol S. & Donald W. Jr. based in Boulder, WY grazes up to 19 horses between May and June and 12 horses between September and November

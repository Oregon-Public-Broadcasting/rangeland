The Dry Creek allotment spans 12,632 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Milo & Richard Mecham based in Carey, ID grazes up to 24 cattle between July and October
  • Jim Or Susan Barton based in Carey, ID grazes up to 71 cattle between July and October
  • Lamar Kyle Drechsel based in Carey, ID grazes up to 18 cattle between July and October
  • Molyneux, John Or William based in Bellevue, ID grazes up to 119 cattle between May and September
  • Dry Creek Cattle Association based in Carey, ID grazes up to 158 cattle between May and September and 168 cattle between July and October
  • Milo Mecham based in Carey, ID grazes up to 27 cattle between July and October

The Laketown allotment spans 3,479 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Roger Earley based in Laketown, UT grazes up to 3 cattle between May and August
  • Bob And Donna Pola Family Revocable Trust based in Bountiful, UT grazes up to 7 cattle between May and August
  • Dean R. Pugmire Family Trust based in Laketown, UT grazes up to 10 cattle between May and August
  • Gwenn P. Dickson Trust based in Pleasant View, UT grazes up to 17 cattle between May and August
  • Kelvin Pugmire based in Laketown, UT grazes up to 50 cattle between May and August

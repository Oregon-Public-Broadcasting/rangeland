The Hohnholz Ranch allotment spans 3,818 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

One ranch uses this land. The one permit issued for this allotment was renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Hohnholz Ranch Inc based in Wheat Ridge, CO grazes up to 3 horses between June and September, 185 cattle between May and July, 25 cattle between June and September, 8 cattle between June and September and 304 cattle between September and October

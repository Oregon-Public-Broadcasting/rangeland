COLORADO

The Square S allotment spans 64,050 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Mantle Ranch based in Rifle, CO grazes up to
    • 80 cattle between March and April, 80 cattle between November and February, 46 cattle between April and July, 140 cattle between July and October, 190 cattle between April and June, 75 cattle between May and July and 250 cattle in October
  • Lov Ranch based in Rifle, CO grazes up to
    • 600 cattle between June and July, 300 cattle between October and December, 100 cattle between March and May, 500 cattle between May and June and 100 cattle between December and February

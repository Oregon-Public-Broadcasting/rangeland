The Rock Canyon (South) allotment spans 2,770 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Dickson Huntington based in Castle Dale, UT grazes up to 52 cattle between May and June and
  • Humphrey, Jay Mark based in Castle Dale, UT grazes up to 197 sheep between November and February
  • Magnuson, Clyde And Darlene based in Castle Dale, UT grazes up to 60 cattle between May and June

The Flat Top allotment spans 35,045 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Simplot Livestock Co based in Grand View, ID grazes up to
    • 459 cattle between March and February
  • Camas Creek Cattle Association Llc based in Fairfield, ID grazes up to
    • 29 cattle between March and February

ARIZONA

The Rock Pockets allotment spans 19,830 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • John R. Brinkerhoff based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 2 horses between March and September, 2 horses between October and February, 91 cattle between October and February and 92 cattle between March and September
  • Baird, Tyler And Vanissa based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 2 horses between March and September, 2 horses between October and February, 67 cattle between October and February and 68 cattle between March and September
  • Baird, Donald A. And Deone M. based in Glendale, UT grazes up to 24 cattle between March and May and 24 cattle between December and February

NEW MEXICO

The West Head Canyon allotment spans 1,403 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Barber, Bessie H. based in Fruitland, NM grazes up to 14 goats between September and February
  • Hemstreet, Minnie based in Farmington, NM grazes up to 14 sheep between September and February
  • Johnson, Margaret H. Estate based in Farmington, NM grazes up to 14 sheep between September and February

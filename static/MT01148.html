MONTANA/DAKOTAS

The West Brazil Creek allotment spans 13,420 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Andrew Schultes based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 63 cattle between June and October
  • Jmc Wesen Partnership based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 149 cattle between May and October and
  • William Jr. Uphaus based in Glasgow, MT grazes up to 3 cattle between June and September and 90 cattle between May and October

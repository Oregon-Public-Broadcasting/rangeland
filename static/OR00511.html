OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Northeast Warner allotment spans 139,019 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jjf Ranch Limited Partnership based in Plush, OR grazes up to 241 cattle between March and August and
  • Jack And Breda Flynn based in Plush, OR grazes up to 241 cattle between March and August, 210 cattle between April and June and
  • Njn Flynn Investments Llc based in Lakeview, OR grazes up to 222 cattle between February and September, 210 cattle between April and June and
  • Fitzgerald Ranch Inc based in Plush, OR grazes up to 180 cattle between March and September and 287 cattle in February

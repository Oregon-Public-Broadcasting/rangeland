The Ragged Top allotment spans 85,920 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • John Espil Sheep Company Inc. based in Sparks, NV grazes up to 2,835 sheep in March and 2,835 sheep between December and February
  • John Olagaray based in Lodi, CA grazes up to 487 sheep between January and February

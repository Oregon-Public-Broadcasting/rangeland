The Sheep Creek allotment spans 3,302 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Lava Lake Land & Livestock Llc based in Hailey, ID grazes up to
    • 945 sheep between May and November
  • Flat Top Grazing Assn based in Carey, ID grazes up to
    • 18 sheep between May and November

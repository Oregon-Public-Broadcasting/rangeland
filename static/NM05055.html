The Pump Mesa allotment spans 28,767 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. The one permit issued for this allotment was not renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Joe & Brad Walck & Herrera based in Hesperus, CO grazes up to 300 cattle between November and February and 300 cattle between March and June

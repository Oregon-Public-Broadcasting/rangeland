COLORADO

The Ward Cr. Doughspoon allotment spans 17,000 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Steve & Julie Coury based in Payson,, AZ grazes up to 25 cattle between October and November,
  • Larry I. Kier based in Delta,, CO grazes up to 226 cattle between May and June and 226 cattle in October
  • Sanburg, Rolf L. & Lynn A. based in Eckert,, CO grazes up to 26 cattle in October and 58 cattle between May and June
  • Sanburg, Lynn A. & Alice M. based in Eckert,, CO grazes up to 11 cattle in October and 21 cattle between May and June
  • Long, Dan And Roy based in Cory,, CO grazes up to 142 cattle between May and June and 90 cattle between October and November

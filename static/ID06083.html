The Inkom allotment spans 5,511 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Stanger, Elmer based in Inkom, ID grazes up to 56 cattle between May and August
  • Mink Creek Spring Grazing Assn based in Arbon, ID grazes up to 350 cattle in May
  • Smith, Kip J based in Inkom, ID grazes up to 57 cattle between June and August and

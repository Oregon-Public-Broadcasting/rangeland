The Toplift-Vernon Hill allotment spans 38,887 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Skyline Sheep Co. Inc based in Mt. Pleasant, UT grazes up to 1,684 sheep between November and February and 1,684 sheep between March and April
  • Larry W. Fitzgerald based in Eureka, UT grazes up to 275 cattle between November and February, 345 sheep between November and February, 275 cattle between March and April and 345 sheep between March and April

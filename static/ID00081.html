The Milk Creek allotment spans 840 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • George & Linda Bates based in Midvale, ID grazes up to 52 cattle between May and July
  • Bill Wilcock based in Weiser, ID grazes up to 86 cattle between May and July and 36 cattle between May and July

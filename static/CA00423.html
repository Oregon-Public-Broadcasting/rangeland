CALIFORNIA

The Tablelands allotment spans 16,052 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Chris And Nancy Miller based in Litchfield, CA grazes up to 60 cattle between April and August
  • Darrell Wood based in Vina, CA grazes up to 204 cattle between April and August
  • Logan And Dallice Nuttall based in Vina, CA grazes up to 204 cattle between April and August

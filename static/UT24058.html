The Harris Flat allotment spans 4,292 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Dirk L. Ballard (Gates/Weber Lease) based in Fredonia, AZ grazes up to 21 cattle between May and October
  • Merlin Gibson (Ballard Lease) based in Virgin, UT grazes up to 22 cattle between May and October
  • Jdtk/Ballard Ranch Llc. based in Hurricane, UT grazes up to 18 cattle between May and October

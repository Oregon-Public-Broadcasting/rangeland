UTAH

The Jackson Wash allotment spans 29,687 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Curtis & Annette Graff based in Washington, UT grazes up to 12 cattle between March and May and 12 cattle between November and February
  • Burgess Ranch, L.L.C. based in St. George, UT grazes up to 108 cattle between November and February and 108 cattle between March and May
  • Grassy Flat Land & Livestock, Llc based in Central, UT grazes up to 59 cattle between November and May
  • Milo Ence And Sons, Llc based in Ivins, UT grazes up to 101 cattle between November and May

The Granny Peak allotment spans 1,932 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Thoman, Mary A. Primary Trust based in Green River, WY grazes up to 33 cattle between May and September
  • Walker, Steve & Phyllis based in Opal, WY grazes up to 31 cattle between May and September
    • 3 horses between May and September and 5 cattle between May and September

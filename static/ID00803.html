The Big Springs allotment spans 187,743 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Sierra Del Rio based in Murphy, ID grazes up to 547 cattle between May and October
  • Joseph Black And Sons based in Bruneau, ID grazes up to 3 horses between April and October, 394 cattle between April and October
  • Joe Black And Sons based in Bruneau, ID grazes up to 219 cattle between April and October
  • J. R. Simplot Co. based in Grand View, ID grazes up to 20 horses between April and September, 1,747 cattle between April and September

The Lakeside allotment spans 20,511 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Vernon Beef Project based in Vernon, UT grazes up to 144 cattle between March and April and 144 cattle between November and February
  • Richard Anderson based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 94 cattle between March and April, 33 cattle between March and April, 33 cattle between November and February and 94 cattle between November and February
  • Browns Diamond J based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 29 cattle between March and April and 29 cattle between November and February
  • Boyer, Wayne And Blaine based in Grantsville, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between November and February and 30 cattle between March and April

The Pine Creek/Indian Creek allotment spans 9,686 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Seven ranches use this land. Of the seven permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Yardley, Joe And Penny based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 30 cattle between May and June
  • Y Ranch Company, Llc based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 45 cattle between May and June
  • Yardley, Lee R. based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 44 cattle between May and June
  • Nowers, William M. And Son based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 28 cattle between May and October
  • Bradshaw Family, H. Clark & Shirley Ltd Pnsp based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 113 cattle between May and October
  • Nowers, Wilson Gates & Cindy based in Richfield, UT grazes up to 27 cattle between May and October
  • Yardley, Darrell based in Beaver, UT grazes up to 31 cattle between May and June

CALIFORNIA

The Chase Valley allotment spans 2,460 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Nor Cal Land & Cattle Co based in San Jose, CA grazes up to 19 cattle between April and May
  • Daran V. & Lynette G. Myers based in Adin,, CA grazes up to 35 cattle between April and May
  • Wynn And Tamsen Myers based in Adin, CA grazes up to 109 cattle between May and June

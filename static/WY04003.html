The Vermillion Creek allotment spans 139,551 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, three were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Vermillion Ranch Limited Partnership based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 463 cattle in September , 332 cattle in October , 792 cattle in April , 228 cattle between November and February, 228 cattle between March and April, 1,044 cattle in March , 131 cattle in June , 1,758 cattle in November , 1,045 cattle between December and February, 315 cattle in May and 794 cattle in October
  • Jock T. Sheehan (Mud Springs Lse) based in Dixon, WY grazes up to 101 cattle in November , 101 cattle between September and October, 86 cattle between April and June, 8 cattle between March and February,
  • Southfork Sheep Company based in Rock Springs, WY grazes up to 3,430 sheep between March and April and 3,430 sheep between November and February

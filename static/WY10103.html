The Cyclone Rim allotment spans 291,954 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jolley Livestock Grazing Association, Llc based in Wamsutter, WY grazes up to 26 cattle between May and December

  • Stratton Sheep Co. based in Shoshoni, WY grazes up to 2,043 cattle between November and February, 2,043 cattle between March and April, 5,930 sheep between October and February and 5,930 sheep between March and April
  • Peterson Livestock, Llc based in Rawlins, WY grazes up to 170 sheep between May and July

The Springfield allotment spans 34,546 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Earl L. Bingham Ranch, Llc based in Honeyville, UT grazes up to 490 cattle between April and June
  • Samuel H Chandler based in Springfield, ID grazes up to 127 cattle between April and June
  • Soren Pierce based in Sterling, ID grazes up to 252 cattle between April and June
  • Larry D. Caldwell based in Pingree, ID grazes up to 237 cattle between April and June
  • Layne Hamilton based in Blackfoot, ID grazes up to 73 cattle between April and June
  • D-2 Land & Livestock, Llc based in Springfield, ID grazes up to 133 cattle between April and June

OREGON/WASHINGTON

The North Rabbit Hills allotment spans 11,712 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Jjf Ranch Limited Partnership based in Plush, OR grazes up to 127 cattle between January and April,
  • Jack And Breda Flynn based in Plush, OR grazes up to 180 cattle between February and April,
  • Njn Flynn Investments Llc based in Lakeview, OR grazes up to 180 cattle between February and April,

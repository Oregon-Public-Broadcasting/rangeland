The Meadow Creek allotment spans 13,372 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, all six were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bronson, Robert D. Dba/ Bronson Sheep based in Burley, ID grazes up to 1,000 sheep between May and August,
  • Parke Ranch based in Malta, ID grazes up to 265 sheep between June and September and 72 cattle between June and September
  • Kevin Tracy based in Malta,, ID grazes up to 265 sheep between June and September and 237 cattle between June and September
  • Wickel, A. Eugene & Wynell based in Malta, ID grazes up to 108 cattle between June and September, 265 sheep between June and September,
  • G. H. Matthews based in Declo, ID grazes up to 265 sheep between June and September, 116 cattle between June and September,
  • Hitt Ranch Llc based in Burley, ID grazes up to 25 cattle between June and September

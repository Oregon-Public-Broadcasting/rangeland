The Grouse Creek allotment spans 35,564 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Big Creek Ranch, Llc based in Ketchum, ID grazes up to 700 cattle between September and October
  • Circle Pi Ranch based in May, ID grazes up to 400 cattle between September and October and 400 cattle between May and June
  • Roy M. Hoffman, Jr. based in May, ID grazes up to 274 cattle between September and October and 274 cattle between May and June

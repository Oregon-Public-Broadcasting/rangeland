The Walcott allotment spans 12,523 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Weber, Jeff & Keri Jo based in Dietrich, ID grazes up to 102 cattle between April and September
  • Whiting Brothers based in Rupert, ID grazes up to 103 cattle between April and September
  • Twin Stock Llc based in Rupert, ID grazes up to 180 cattle between April and September

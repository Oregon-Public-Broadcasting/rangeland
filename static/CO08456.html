COLORADO

The Vigil-Abeyta Mesas allotment spans 712 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Martinez, Albert J. based in Chromo, CO grazes up to 3 cattle between June and September
  • Chavez, Alice Or Eddie based in Taylorsville, UT grazes up to 20 cattle between June and September
  • Abeyta, Joe P. based in Los Ojos, NM grazes up to 10 cattle between June and September
  • Talamante, Johnny based in Chromo, CO grazes up to 18 cattle between June and September

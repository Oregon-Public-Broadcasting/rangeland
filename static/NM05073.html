NEW MEXICO

The Jaquez Community allotment spans 12,757 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Eight ranches use this land. Of the eight permits issued for this allotment, seven were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Blackie, Frank based in Blanco, NM grazes up to 42 sheep between March and February
  • Benny, Robert based in Blanco, NM grazes up to 7 cattle between March and February
  • Seeley, Laverne & Antonito, Eunice based in Farmington, NM grazes up to 17 sheep between March and April and 17 sheep between October and February
  • Victor, Samuel based in Bloomfield, NM grazes up to 6 sheep between March and February
  • Yazzie, Ethel B. & Thomas Bunnie based in Blanco, NM grazes up to 15 sheep between March and February
  • Valdez, Murphy Or Anna based in Bloomfield, NM grazes up to 36 sheep between March and February
  • Antonito, Melvin based in Bloomfield, NM grazes up to 7 cattle between March and February
  • Aaron Betselie based in Bloomfield, NM grazes up to 12 cattle between June and November

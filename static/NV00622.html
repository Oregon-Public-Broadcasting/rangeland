NEVADA

The Warm Springs Trl allotment spans 16,385 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Tumbling Jr Ranch based in Elko, NV grazes up to 4,700 sheep in November , 4,600 sheep in March ,
  • The Little Paris Sheep Company, Llc based in Jiggs, NV grazes up to 1,040 sheep between November and December, 1,040 sheep between April and May,

The Indian Valley allotment spans 2,150 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Three ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • J W & Son Holmes based in Indian Valley, ID grazes up to 155 cattle between October and November, 175 cattle between May and July and 10 cattle between July and August
  • Ralph L Yantis based in Indian Valley, ID grazes up to 53 cattle between May and August
  • Gerald L. Baker Trust based in Vale, OR grazes up to 58 cattle between October and November

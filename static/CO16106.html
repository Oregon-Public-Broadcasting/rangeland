The East Toms Can Comm allotment spans 3,680 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Anne Roehm Lawson based in Glade Park, CO grazes up to 60 cattle between April and June, 60 cattle between November and December,
  • Bieser Creek Cattle based in Glade Park, CO grazes up to 37 cattle in May and 100 cattle in October

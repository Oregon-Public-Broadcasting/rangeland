COLORADO

The Wilbanks allotment spans 3,230 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Seymour, Earl W. & Jean R. based in Olathe,, CO grazes up to 120 cattle in October and 105 cattle between May and June
  • Troy D. & Lorie A. Spencer based in Hotchkiss,, CO grazes up to 50 cattle in October and 50 cattle between May and June
  • Hebenstreit, Gordon &Cheryl based in Cedaredge,, CO grazes up to 35 cattle in October
  • Vaughan Ranches, Inc. based in Cedaredge,, CO grazes up to 101 cattle in October and 101 cattle between May and June
  • Vela, James D. & Carol based in Eckert,, CO grazes up to 62 cattle in October and 76 cattle between May and June
  • Ron K. Suprise based in Mcallen, TX grazes up to 115 cattle in October

OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Jackies Butte Summer allotment spans 208,277 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment had not been evaluated for BLM's rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Six ranches use this land. Of the six permits issued for this allotment, all six were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Dowell Brothers based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 200 cattle between April and October, 102 cattle between April and October, 47 cattle between April and October and 336 cattle between April and October
  • Richmar Llc based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 407 cattle between April and October
  • Berrett Land Llc based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 239 cattle between April and October and 1 cattle between September and October
  • Grenke Ranches based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 76 cattle between April and October and 1 cattle between June and October
  • Matteri, James & Sons based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 512 cattle between April and October and 1 cattle in October
  • Jesse & Pamela White based in Jordan Valley, OR grazes up to 55 cattle between April and October

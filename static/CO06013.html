COLORADO

The Fourteen Mile allotment spans 2,461 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Two ranches use this land. Of the two permits issued for this allotment, none were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Ellen Cox based in Rifle, CO grazes up to 19 cattle between June and September and 5 cattle between October and January
  • Wade Cox based in Rangely, CO grazes up to 65 cattle between October and January

The North Ferron allotment spans 7,370 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, all four were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Behling, Lisa Revocable Family Trust based in Ferron, UT grazes up to 23 cattle between March and June and 23 cattle between November and December
  • Singleton, Samuel based in Ferron, UT grazes up to 190 cattle between November and December and 107 cattle between March and June
  • Lemon, John & Sheila K. based in Ferron, UT grazes up to 24 cattle between March and June and 24 cattle between November and December
  • Mcelprang, Lee Or Leon based in Huntington, UT grazes up to 36 cattle between November and December, 36 cattle between March and June,

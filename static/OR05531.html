OREGON/WASHINGTON

The Stinkingwater allotment spans 23,694 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Livestock grazing was cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the three permits issued for this allotment, one were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Marcus & Polly O'Toole based in Drewsey, OR grazes up to 182 cattle between December and February and
  • Miler Ranch Llc based in Drewsey, OR grazes up to 328 cattle between April and September and

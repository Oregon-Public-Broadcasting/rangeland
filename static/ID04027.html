The Goose Creek Group allotment spans 28,730 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment did not meet rangeland health standards in its last evaluation. Current livesotck grazing was not cited as a factor.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Five ranches use this land. Of the five permits issued for this allotment, all five were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Bruce Bedke based in Oakley, ID grazes up to 323 cattle between May and October
  • Michael A. Poulton based in Oakley, ID grazes up to 80 cattle in May
  • Pickett Ranch And Sheep Company based in Oakley, ID grazes up to 254 sheep between May and June and 1,015 sheep between May and June
  • Hawker Craig And Renae based in Oakley, ID grazes up to 77 cattle between May and October and 364 cattle between May and October
  • Jones, R.O. And Sons Inc. based in Almo, ID grazes up to 122 cattle between May and October

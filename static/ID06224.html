The Timber Creek allotment spans 7,066 acres

Allotment health and management

As of 2013, the most recent year of available data, this grazing allotment was meeting rangeland health standards.

The BLM has no listed allotment management plan, which would set long-term goals for rangeland improvement and outline specific monitoring plans.

Who uses this allotment

Four ranches use this land. Of the four permits issued for this allotment, two were renewed under a provision in federal law that exempts permits from environmental review.

  • Beyeler Ranches L.L.C. based in Leadore, ID grazes up to
    • 5 horses between May and October, 245 cattle between May and June and 245 cattle in October
  • Whittaker, James And Paula based in Leadore, ID grazes up to 1,200 sheep between May and June
  • Steven And Susan Johnson based in Leadore, ID grazes up to 100 cattle between May and June
  • Allan Purcell based in Leadore, ID grazes up to 75 cattle between May and June
